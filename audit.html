The AI Reputation Audit™

A comprehensive, evidence-based assessment of how an entity is represented across search engines and AI platforms — the starting point for every engagement Silicon Minds delivers.

Request a Reputation Assessment

Purpose

Why the audit exists.

Most organizations can describe their reputation in general terms but cannot quantify it. The AI Reputation Audit™ closes that gap. It establishes a defensible, evidence-based baseline — a Digital Trust Score™ — so that leadership can make decisions about reputation with the same rigor applied to any other strategic asset.

Methodology

How the assessment is conducted.

01

Digital footprint mapping

A full inventory of the entity's presence across search, social, professional, and structured-data platforms.

02

AI platform analysis

Direct queries against ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity to document exactly how each platform represents the entity today.

03

Search reputation review

A structured review of sentiment, accuracy, and composition across the organic search results page.

04

Framework scoring

Every finding is scored against the seven pillars of the Digital Trust Framework™ and weighted into a single score.

Scoring

A single, weighted Digital Trust Score™.

Each of the seven pillars is scored on a 100-point scale and weighted by structural importance — AI Visibility and Search Reputation at 20% each, Executive Authority, Entity Authority and Media Presence at 15% each, Digital Trust Signals at 10%, and Risk Assessment at 5%. The result is a single number, comparable across time.
