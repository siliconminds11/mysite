How We Work

A disciplined, five-stage process.

Every engagement follows the same sequence — from assessment to sustained monitoring — so that progress is measurable and results are durable.

STAGE 01

Assessment

The AI Reputation Audit™ establishes the Digital Trust Score™ baseline — a full inventory of the digital footprint, direct queries against the major AI platforms, and a structured review of search reputation. Nothing in the roadmap that follows is speculative; every recommendation traces back to a documented finding from this stage.

STAGE 02

Framework

Findings are mapped against the seven pillars of the Digital Trust Framework™ and scored. This produces a weighted Digital Trust Score™ and, more importantly, a ranked view of where the largest gaps between current state and desired state exist.

STAGE 03

Roadmap

Prioritized findings are translated into a four-quarter implementation plan — sequenced so that foundational work precedes visibility-building work, which in turn precedes long-term consolidation.

STAGE 04

Implementation

Silicon Minds executes the roadmap directly, in partnership with your team — spanning Authority Building™, Entity & Knowledge Graph Optimization™, Strategic Digital PR™, and the other solutions the roadmap calls for.

STAGE 05

Monitoring

Representation across search and AI platforms is tracked continuously. Results are reported quarterly against the original baseline, so progress is never a matter of impression.

Begin with Stage One.

Every engagement starts with the AI Reputation Audit™.
