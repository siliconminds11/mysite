Our Methodology

The Digital Trust Framework™

A proprietary, weighted methodology that converts a wide, fragmented set of digital signals into a single, defensible measure of trust — the foundation of every engagement Silicon Minds delivers.

How It Works

Seven pillars. One weighted score.

Each pillar is assessed independently, scored on a 100-point scale, and weighted according to its structural importance to overall Digital Trust. The result is the Digital Trust Score™ — a single number that is comparable across time and across entities.

PILLAR 01 · WEIGHT 20%

AI Visibility

Measures how accurately and consistently ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity describe the entity — including factual accuracy, completeness, and consistency across platforms. This is the pillar most organizations have never measured.

PILLAR 02 · WEIGHT 20%

Search Reputation

Assesses the sentiment, accuracy, and composition of the organic search results page — the surface that has historically defined online reputation, and remains foundational.

PILLAR 03 · WEIGHT 15%

Executive Authority

Evaluates the strength, accuracy, and verifiability of professional credentials, titles, and positioning as represented across the web.

PILLAR 04 · WEIGHT 15%

Entity Authority

Examines how precisely the entity is represented within structured data and the Knowledge Graph — the layer that disambiguates identity for both search engines and AI platforms.

PILLAR 05 · WEIGHT 15%

Media Presence

Measures the volume, quality, and credibility of third-party, earned media coverage — a signal that AI platforms weight heavily as external validation.

PILLAR 06 · WEIGHT 10%

Digital Trust Signals

Assesses verification markers — citations, structured data, endorsements, and reviews — that corroborate an entity's claims across the digital ecosystem.

PILLAR 07 · WEIGHT 5%

Risk Assessment

Identifies exposure to outdated, conflicting, or unverified information that could erode trust — the pillar most directly tied to Digital Crisis Response™.

From Framework to Score

The Digital Trust Score™

Each pillar score is multiplied by its weight and summed into a single Digital Trust Score™ out of 100 — the metric used to establish a baseline, track progress, and report results to leadership every quarter.
